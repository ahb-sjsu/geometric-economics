# The geometric decision program — a body of work

A foundation, not a series. One central claim, stated as mathematics and discharged by empirics, each
piece written to stand alone and held to exactly the confidence its evidence supports. The ambition is
in the *sharpness of the claim* and the *rigor of the apparatus* — never in inflated results.

## The central claim

> Economic choice is navigation on a low-dimensional decision manifold. Scalar utility is an
> information-losing projection of it. One shared metric governs both risky and strategic choice, and
> there exist regimes where that metric predicts behavior no scalar theory can express — signed, out of
> sample, and invariant to how the researcher encodes the attributes.

## Structure

| Piece | Role | Discharges | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Paper 0 — Theory (keystone)** | the claim as theorems: scalar irrecoverability, projection-nesting, the projection-gap, encoding-invariance | states the obligations; separates the necessary (empty) from the contentful | `paper_00_theory.md` — draft |
| **Paper 1 — Cross-domain (pillar)** | one metric, calibrated on risk, predicts strategy | **parsimony** | `paper_01_cross_domain.md` — draft; results run |
| **Paper 2 — Projection-gap (pillar)** | the falsification instrument + LLM model-organism evidence | **the projection gap** (on a model organism) | `paper_02_projection_gap.md` — draft; panel run |
| **Capstone — Human study** | the confirmatory human projection-gap + the redesigned rule | converts the strict-subsumption corollary into a fact about human behavior | designed (`prereg-v2`); not yet run |

## The load-bearing honesty (what makes a foundation)

Each piece names what it does **not** establish:

- Paper 1 ties CPT on lotteries (does not beat it) and transfers only the *risk* part of the metric,
  not the social coordinates; level-k does not help.
- Paper 2 is a **model-organism** result (language models, not humans); two pre-registered predictions
  failed (loss reversal; dose shape) and are reported, not buried; the failures localized to the
  temperature, which was redesigned and re-registered.
- Paper 0's nesting theorems are necessary and therefore **scientifically empty on their own**; the
  content is entirely in the two empirical obligations.

A foundation is only as strong as the honesty of its weakest claim. Every prediction here was frozen
and hashed before data (`prereg-v1` scored; `prereg-v2` for the human study); every competitor was
judged under a common protocol; every dead end is on the record.

## The path to the decisive result

The program is *pointed* at one experiment: the **human projection-gap** under the redesigned rule,
powered by Paper 2's effect sizes (d ≈ 0.5–0.9), with encoding-invariant signs and pre-committed
falsifiers. That result — not any paper here — is what would move the work from "a rigorous, honest,
field-relevant reframing of decision theory" toward the kind of decisive, adopted phenomenon that
reshapes a field. The papers are the solid ground it stands on.
